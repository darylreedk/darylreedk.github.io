IOS Productivity App

This app is designed to help keep the easily distracted on track. It is a typical todo/note app and utilizes an updating lock-screen banner notification that shows the next task the user has to complete on their todo list. The motivation behind this app is that many of my peers try to use their phone as a tool but get easily sidetracked by the many distractions they contain. By keeping the todo list on the lock screen, it allows them to stay focused without a greater risk of distraction.
